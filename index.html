About Our School

Welcome to Global School & College System

Established in the heart of Sohan, Islamabad, Global School & College System has been providing quality education from Pre-Year 1 to 12th Grade for over a decade. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow.

We believe in holistic development that combines academic excellence with character building, ensuring our students become responsible global citizens.

Our Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive educational programs from early childhood to higher secondary education.

Early Years (Pre-Year 1 to 2)

Our play-based learning approach helps young children develop social, emotional and cognitive skills in a nurturing environment.

  • Phonics & Literacy
  • Numeracy Skills
  • Creative Arts

Primary (Grade 1 to 5)

Building strong foundations in core subjects while encouraging curiosity and independent thinking.

  • English, Urdu, Math
  • General Science
  • Social Studies
  • Islamic Studies

Secondary (Grade 6 to 12)

Preparing students for board examinations and higher education with specialized subject streams.

  • Science Stream
  • Commerce Stream
  • Arts/Humanities
  • Computer Science

Our Facilities

We provide modern facilities to support our students' learning and development.

Science Labs

Well-equipped physics, chemistry and biology laboratories for practical learning experiences.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with internet access to develop digital literacy and IT skills.

Library

Extensive collection of books, reference materials and periodicals to encourage reading.

Sports Facilities

Playground and indoor sports facilities for physical development and team sports.
